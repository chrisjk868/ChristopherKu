Personal Projects

PresqueOut

Prototyped, built and implemented a fullstack E - learning platform called PresqueOut for the Hong Kong City I & T Grand Challenge. Allows new users to sign up and login to our service and browse relevant course informations posted on our website by using Heroku as a hosting service, Node.js as our back-end and MongoDB as the database.

HomeSurf

Implemented a Hotel Reservation Site called HomeSurf which enables users to sign-up and login to their accounts and make reservations according to their search queries and filter options. Built a dynamic service by writing my own API, and using SQLite as a database to store information on different hotels. In addition, integrated the Google Maps and Recommendations API for location and landmark suggestions.
